Referral Bonus Program

We will pay you a referral bonus if you refer a person who is qualified for one of our openings and, within six months of your referral of a person with the listed skills who was previously unknown to us, we:

  • Place the individual as one of our hourly consultants and they remain on contract with us for at least 90 days, or
  • Hire the individual as a salaried staff consultant and they complete a 90 day introductory period, or
  • Place the individual as an employee of one of our clients and they complete a 90 day introductory period with the client.
