Who are we?

We are a young and progressive consulting company based in Los Angeles, California with the goal of making the hiring process more efficient and less time-consuming. As a committed business to business operator we specialize in placing IT professionals into exciting positions throughout the area.

learn more

Why Choose GotLink?


In today’s fast paced society it’s getting more and more difficult to find and hire quality professionals who are also the right people for your company. This is why hiring managers employ all available resources in the market, using the services of outside consulting firms even if they have a dedicated HR department in their companies. Making the hiring process more cost-effective and less time-consuming is the main goal of our business and a major benefit for our partners.

What We Provide

  • Contract, Contract-To-Hire, and Direct Placement options
  • Skills Testing
  • Professional employment and personal reference checks
  • Degree and certification validation
