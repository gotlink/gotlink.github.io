Types of employment available:

  • Contract Positions
    These are for individuals who are looking for exiting, fast-paced career, with variety and exposure to the vast range of technologies. The projects are relatively short, from couple of months up to an year.
  • Contract-to-Hire Positions
    Best for situations in which you are not decided yet that you want to stay in a given company. It is always wise to check up first the working environment and management’ compatibility, before you make a long-term commitment.
  • Direct Placement
    This type of employment usually goes best when the position is in well known, established company with excellent reputation, that you are certain you want to be part of.
