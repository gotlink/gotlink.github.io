Why GotLink?

In today’s fast paced society it’s getting more and more difficult to find and hire quality professionals, who are also the right people for your company. This is why hiring managers employ all available resources in the market, using services of outside recruitment firms, even if they have HR department in their companies. Making the hiring process more cost-effective and less time-consuming is the main goal of our business and a major benefit for our partners.

Using outside recruiting services makes sense when you need fast results. It is not efficient business practice to have your key personnel postpone their current duties and projects, in order to screen resumes, do background checks and schedule interviews. The workflow in your company will continue smoothly when you rely on professional and experienced outside partners.

What we provide:

  • Contract, contract-to-hire, and direct placement options
  • Skills testing
  • Professional employment and personal reference checks
  • Degree and certification validation
  • Personality and psychological assessments
